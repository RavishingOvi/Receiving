Receiving Team
Major Function: Receives Shipment from Outbound and VDO
Receiving Operations

Handling inbound shipments with accuracy and efficiency

01

Shipment Intake

Receives shipment from Outbound and VDO channels.

02

DA Reconciliation

Verification and reconciliation from DA (FMH) systems.

03

Bay Scanning

Physical scan and verification of large items directly from the bay.

04

HMT Receiving

Formal system receiving using Handheld Mobile Terminals.

05

Tier Sorting

Pre-sorting items based on designated Tiers for efficiency.

06

Team Handover

Final handover to the sorting team for processing.

Process Steps

  • Reconciliation from DA (FMH)



  • Scan large items from the bay



  • Receive on HMT



  • Pre sort based on Tiers



  • Handover to the sorting team



  • Escalate when shipment is not complete on pre-alert mail



  • Follow up on aged in-transit items in queue



⚠️

Escalation Protocol

Escalate immediately whenever a shipment is not complete based on the pre-alert mail. Follow up on the aged in transit items on the queue daily.
